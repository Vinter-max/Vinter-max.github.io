Hololive

Hololive is an idol group like AKB48. It is an agency of Virtual YouTubers created by Cover Corps. Yagoo. As of November 2019, the total number of subscribers for the VTubers on YouTube and bilibili are 2.6 million and 3.5 million, respectively, with four of the VTubers getting into the top 10 spots for VTubers on bilibili: Shirakami Fubuki (2nd), Minato Aq-ua (5th), Natsuiro Matsuri (7th), and Akai Haato (9th).
